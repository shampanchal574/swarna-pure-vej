Our Story

A Taste of Home
in the Heart of Warje

Born from a simple belief that pure vegetarian food can be extraordinary, Swarnaa Vegetarian has become one of Pune's most-loved restaurants — earning 6,000+ glowing reviews and a loyal community in Giridhar Nagar, Warje.

  • Freshly sourced ingredients every morning — no shortcuts, ever.
  • From sizzling South Indian dosas to hearty North Indian curries, all under one roof.
  • Highest hygiene standards — our kitchen is always inspection-ready.
  • Serving Warje families since day one with love and consistency.
  • Affordable pricing that never compromises on quality or taste.
